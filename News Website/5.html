Corporate India leaders write to Reserve Bank governor to lower interest rates to kickstart the economy

Written by Nomaan Attarwala , March 29, 2021 - 9:00
Business

Starting from the demonetisation, the Indian economy has been on a downward spiral. To counter this the leaders of corporate India, including Vijaya Lakshmi. T. K. Patel and Som Nagda, in a letter requested the RBI governor Mr. Khan to help industry tied over the situation with lower interest rates.
