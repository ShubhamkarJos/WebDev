Ambani's youngest son gets married to his childhood sweetheart today at a grand ceremony at their residence

Bollywood stars, industry leaders, politicians and people from the family's ancestral village Chorwad attend.

Written by Dhanush B. Sachin , March 29, 2021 - 16:00
Entertainment

Ambani’s youngest son Guddu Ambani gets married to his childhood sweetheart Pinky Oberoi, daughter of Sanjay Oberoi who owns the famous Oberoi group of hotels. Alok Ambani, popularly known as Guddu heads the petrochemical division of Reliance while Pinky, Astha Oberoi heads the Oberoi chain of hotels of Europe Division.

All the who’s who of India’s corporate world, bankers, political leaders and Bollywood celebrities dazzled the ceremony by their presence. The villagers from Ambani’s ancestral village chorwad also came all the way to attend the Grand ceremony, inspite of the pandemic restrictions.
