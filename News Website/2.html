Prime Minister Modi launched the third vaccine for India today in Delhi.

Written by Kabeer. March 29, 2021
Covid-19

Prime Minister Narendra Modi launches the third vaccine ‘Coalax’ today through an online meeting today in New Delhi. Coalax, developed by the serum institute of India in Pune will soon be made available in all government and private hospitals across India, says Modi during the launch. The vaccine will be suitable for people over the age of 50 and will be made available gradually as the vaccination stages progress. “This is an important stride in India’s battle against the Coronavirus” says Modi during his launch speech.
